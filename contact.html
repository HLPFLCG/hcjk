Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for weddings and major events. For portraits and smaller sessions, 2-4 weeks notice is usually sufficient.

What is included in a photography package?

Our packages typically include coverage time, edited digital images, online gallery, and printing rights. Specific inclusions vary by package type.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! We love traveling for destination weddings and special sessions. Travel fees apply for locations outside our local area.
